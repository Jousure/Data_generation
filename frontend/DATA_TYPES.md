## **How to Use**

When describing your dataset in the application, you can reference any of these data types. For example:

- "Generate customer data with first name, last name, email address, and phone number"
- "Create a product catalog with product name, category, price, and description"
- "Generate flight data with departure airport, arrival airport, airline code, and flight duration"
- "Create hospital records with patient names, diagnosis codes, and treatment procedures"

The system will automatically understand these terms and generate appropriate mock data for each field.
